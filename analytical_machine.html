Analytical Machine

Machines

Dates: 1450-1840

Why Significant

Intended as a general symbol manipulator, the Analytical Engine was a flexible and powerful punched-card-controlled calculator, embodying many features which later reappeared in the modern stored-program computer.

History

The Analytical Engine was another machine envisioned by Babbage in the 19th century. He intended it to be a general-purpose computing device.
