Linus Torvalds

Dates: Born December 28, 1969

Why Significant

Linus Torvalds created the Linux Kernel. This opened the doors of open-source and community collaboration. He is mainly known for creating Linux kernel and showing the world how powerful open-source software can be.

History

Torvalds was born in Helsinki, Finland. He attended University of Helsinki in 1991. Linux's first version came out in 1991 as well. Torvalds then moved to the United states in 1994 to work for Silicon Valley-based Transmeta Corporation.
