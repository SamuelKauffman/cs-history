Biography

Edsger Dijkstra was an early pioneer and giant figure in computer science. He was a theoretical physicist by training but he worked as a programmer early in his career at Mathematisch Centrum in Amsterdam in the 1950s. He contributed to the discipline in numerous areas including: Compiler construction, Operating Systems, Concurrent Programming, Distribute Programming.
